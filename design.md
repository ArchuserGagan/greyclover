# GreyCloverLabs Design.md

## Core Brand Concept

A business is a living system.

Knowledge exists everywhere:

* Documents
* SOPs
* Meetings
* Employee experience
* Internal processes

Most companies lose time because this knowledge is fragmented.

GreyCloverLabs builds Company Brains that transform scattered information into an accessible intelligence layer.

The rotating matrix cube represents organizational knowledge in motion.
